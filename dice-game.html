How to Play

Dice Game Instructions

Roll two dice and compete against the computer for 3 rounds.

  • If you roll a 1, your score for that round is 0.
  • Rolling a pair doubles your score for that round.
  • Otherwise, your score is the sum of the dice.

After 3 rounds, the player with the highest total score wins!

Click Roll Dice to play and Reset to start over.
